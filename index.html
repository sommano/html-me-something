Intro

The world of Azeroth is an awesome place to visit. It is a fantasy world where Orcs and humans can coexist peacefully or in total warfare with each other. Azeroth contains many demon and monsters to battle for glory and spoils of war. Some enemies are easy while others take an entire army to take down. Some bad guys are big, bad, and mean to the good guys. It takes skill and power to overcome these enemies. But once these bosses fall, you can earn reputation, gold, treasure, and glory from this acheivement.

One of the Planets in this fantasy world is Azeroth. There are 5 continents that one can adventure in the world of Azeroth. There are dungeons and raids that contain enemies inside each of these continents. The Orcish horde dominate the continent of Kalimdor, while the humans occupy the Eastern Kingdom. Pandas come from the land of Pandaria. Pandarans are the master race of Azeroth and are the best race on Azeroth.

Training

I am Surferdude, a Pandaren mage from the Wandering Isle. It is an island located on a giant turtle that swims around the oceans of Azeroth

All characters in the World of Warcraft are born and start out as lowly peasants. We begin life at level 1 and need to work our way up to level 110. This journey is long and hard and many die along the way or simply run out of gametime. It is the survival of the fittest.

First one character must choose a class from a list of 12. Each class has different abilities and spells. Some are stronger with swords and some are better at magic and spells. There are 3 roles one can play in this adventure. A tank, DPS, or a healer. A tank is the strongest of the group and charges head on to an enemy. They are responsible for getting the attention of enemies and protecting them from the other party members. A dps is someone who deals damage to enemies and stands in fire. A healer is a medical specialist whom may help out other party members with bandages if needed. One can adventure out in the world as a solo player. but it is easier to play as a group of 5 people in a dungeon. Some bigger bosses in a raid may require a group of strong people of 10-40 group members.

Once your class is chosen, u need to acquire a basic weapon. The higher ur level the better weapons u can equip. The leveling process consists of completing quests to level up. Enemies and bosses do earn u a net of experience points to level up also. The fastest way to level up is to run thru dungeons with 5 of ur best friends. This journey could take u over 900 1/5 + quests and/or over 189.722+ dungeon runs to earn level 110.

Journey to the Antorus Raid

Before any adventurer can begin the Quest to beat the Antorus Raid one must first prepare for battle. One must first upgrade all your weapons and battle armor. It is recommended to have all items bea level rating of 930+ ilvl to survive the punishment that these bosses deal. One also must get food and inventory to live thru the Boss encounters. An adventurer cannot venture out hungary and without food for an empty stomach.

The Antorus Raid is the seat of Power for the evil Legion forces. They have killed countless innocent dogs and cats. They stole candy from babies. They must be eliminated because they are evil and we are the good guys. The Council of Six of Dalaran have given us this quest to defeat the Legion forces at the Burning Throne of Antorus. The space ship Vindacaar will fly us to Antorus and drop us off to battle the bosses.

Fighting the Bosses

Each boss in the Antorus Raid requires skill and coordination. This raid requiresa group of 10-30 group members. A raid requires 2 tanks, 1 heal for every 5 people and the rest are DPS. A typical raid 15 person composition could contain 2 tanks, 3 healers and 10 ranged and melee dps.

Boss Fight #1: Garothi Worldbreaker.

The first boss in our way is Garothi Worldbreaker. It is a robot constructed inside the core of the planet to decimate worlds.

Boss Fight #2: Felhounds of Sargeras

The 2nd fight has bosses infused with fire and shadow, F’harg and Shatug are the prized pets of Sargeras. Bred for carnage, these hounds delight in eviscerating their master’s enemies.

Boss Fight #3: Antoran High Command

The Antoran High Command is comprised of 3 bosses Admiral Svirax, Chief Engineer Ishkar, and General Erodus.

Boss Fight #4: Portal Keeper Hasabel

Portal Keeper Hasabel maintains the portal nexus through which the Legion has unleashed its insidious armies upon countless worlds. This boss is capable of bringing the might of the Legion's arsenal to bear upon any who oppose her.

Boss Fight #5: Eonar

Eonar is a titan that we must protect during this fight. We must aid her by destroying any enenmies that try to kill her in this encounter.

Boss Fight #6: Imonar the Soulhunter

The infamous Imonar servers as the Legion's bloodhound, capable of tracking prey across the cosmos. With a vast array of gadgets and traps at his disposal, Imonar has yet to lose a bounty... and he certainly doesn't intend to sully his perfect record by failing to fulfil his latest contract.

Boss Fight #7: Kin'garoth

Kin'garoth is the seventh boss in Antorus, the Burning Throne. Kin'garoth is obsessed with crafting implements of death. He infused his very body into the production machinery so he could more efficiently churn out a never-ending supply of destruction.

Boss Fight #8: Coven of Shivarra

The Coven of Shivarra is the third to last boss encounter housed within Antorus, the Burning Throne. Having proven themselves to be the most depraved and fanatical of all his followers, Sargeras entrusted the Coven with the unholy task of twisting the minds of the fallen Titans into an unstoppable Dark Pantheon. Each sister employs her own signature brand of torture, eliciting screams which echo throughout the halls of Antorus. It is said that neither mortal nor demon can refuse their whispers, making it only a matter of time before the will of the Titans is broken.

Boss Fight #9: Varimathras

As one of the dreadlords overseeing the Legion's forces during the Third War, Varimathras failed to stop the rebellious Lich King. He submitted to an ill-fated alliance with Sylvanas Windrunner, then made one last bid for power before facing a humiliating defeat.

Boss Fight #10: Aggramar

Aggramar is the penultimate boss encountered in Antorus, the Burning Throne. He was once the noble Avenger of the Pantheon, but was struck down by Sargeras and reborn as a titan of destruction.

Boss Fight #11: Argus

The biggest bad boss of the raid is Argus. Bound. Broken. Eons of existence, knowing only pain. A shattered soul, fueling infinite evil. The master beckons. Rise... Rise! Begin, the end of all things.

Quest Completed

When the final boss Argus of Antorus is defeated the quest is finally completed. A long hard journey as been made with many friends and adventurers. The defeat of the boss allows all of us Adventurers a chance to earn a bird from the Council of Six. It is called the Violet Spellwing Raven. It is a reward from the quest to defeat Argus from our Quest givers Council. However treasures and gold isn't the main reason the raid was fun. It is the journey and fun of the group accomplishment that is why we continue to go on adventures.
